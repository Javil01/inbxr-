Frequently asked questions

What's the difference between Toolkit and Intelligence?

Toolkit gives you the Signal Engine and every utility on demand — point it at a domain or upload a CSV whenever you need an answer. Intelligence is the subscription that adds continuous watching: Signal Watch runs every 6 hours, Early Warning alerts fire when signals drift, and Mailchimp suppressions actually propagate. Toolkit buyers can add Intelligence any time without losing their lifetime Toolkit.

Can I stack more than 3 codes?

No. The Toolkit maxes out at T3 (3 codes). Beyond that, additional code purchases would stay dormant and waste your money. If you need more capacity than T3 provides, add the Intelligence subscription instead — it's cheaper and gives you unlimited everything.

What if AppSumo closes the deal later? Do I lose access?

No. Your codes are redeemed to your InbXr account and the Toolkit is yours for the lifetime of InbXr. If AppSumo pulls the listing, existing buyers keep everything they already have.

Lifetime of what? Toolkit features, or everything InbXr builds?

Lifetime of the current Toolkit feature set, plus all future Toolkit features we ship. New Intelligence features (anything that requires continuous watching or ongoing cost) will be added to the MRR tier, not Toolkit. This is standard AppSumo practice and we'll always be upfront about which bucket a new feature lands in.

Can I refund the codes?

Yes, through AppSumo's standard 60-day money-back guarantee. If you refund, your Toolkit access reverts to Free and your redeemed codes return to AppSumo's pool.

Will you publish the code to inbxr.us one day for free?

No. The Toolkit is the lifetime product. The free tier on inbxr.us is a 5-signal CSV preview — useful, but the full 7-signal reading, PDF reports, and utilities are paid. AppSumo buyers get the lifetime version of the paid tier.
