How It Works

Three steps. Under two minutes. No account needed.

1

Get Your Test Address

Click "Start Email Test" and we generate a unique token and seed address. The token lets us find your email in our mailbox.

2

Send From Your System

Send your email from your actual ESP, mail server, or email client — the same way you'd send to your real list. Add the token to your subject line.

3

Get Your Full Report

We pull your email from the receiving server and analyze everything: authentication verdicts, transport security, content quality, reputation, and a prioritized action plan.
